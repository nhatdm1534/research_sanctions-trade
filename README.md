# research_sanctions-trade

This repository contains the code used to replicate the quantitative analysis of an international economics research project on economic sanctions and international trade.

## Overview

The project consists of two main empirical chapters:

### Chapter 3
A quasi-experimental study on the causal effects of economic sanctions on trade flows, implemented within a staggered Difference-in-Differences framework. The analysis employs:
- Two-Way Fixed Effects (TWFE)
- Extended TWFE
- Callaway & Sant'Anna (2021) estimator

### Chapter 4
A quantitative analysis of the role of economic sanctions in moderating the relationship between trade intensity and business cycle comovement. The empirical strategy includes:
- Method of Moments Quantile Regression
- Complete Subset Averaging Two-Stage Least Squares (CSA-2SLS)
